Our Coaches

Shannon Young

A Level 3 High Performance Cricket Australia accredited Coach, Shannon is regarded as one of cricket’s most modern and innovative Coaches. As the Founder of YoungGun Cricket, he has changed the way the game is coached and played focusing on player centric skill acquisition and performance evaluation to create one of the world’s leading state of the art cricket training facilities.

Shannon has a wealth of coaching experience in High Performance, Premier Cricket and local Club environments and is widely respected for his passion and inherent knowledge that he has shared with international players the calibre of Cameron White, Dan Christian, Molly Strano and Scott Edwards among others.

In his 9th season as Head Coach of Richmond CC, he is also responsible for the holistic development of what many believe is Australia’s best collection of young cricket talent and he also plays a key role in the development of the likes of Jake Fraser-McGurk, Wil Parker and Mitch Perry, the next generation of Victorian Cricket.

While a traditionalist at heart, Shannon is also a disruptor and has embraced the modern age of cricket and has been the catalyst of thinking outside the square when creating a hybrid approach to ensuring the basic foundations of cricket are merged with the new age of the game.

Chris Hall

With more than 20 years of experience, Chris Hall has built a reputation as one of the most passionate and knowledgeable coaches in the cricket world.

A Cricket Australia Level 3 Coach, he has held Premier coaching positions with Casey South Melbourne, Richmond, Frankston and Kingston Hawthorn. Most recently, Chris has been Head Coach of Cricket Victoria YPL Region South-East Bayside Breakers.

Drawing on a background in computer science, Chris is at the forefront of Cricket Data Science, equipment performance analysis and technology application for skill acquisition and game development.

Through his quest to revolutionise cricket retail via Cricket World Moorabbin, Chris immersed himself in the future of data driven coaching and performance analysis. A self-confessed Cricket Geek, Chris became an expert in bat sensor technology, swing analytics, Artificial Intelligence Motion Analysis to create Australia’s first custom cricket bat fitting experience.
